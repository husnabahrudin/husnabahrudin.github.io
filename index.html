Managed Services

Our managed services provides a secure, end-to-end and fuly managed on site equipment services. The service is scalable and tailored to your requirement and comes with 24x7 monitoring and support.

Customer Premise Equipment (CPE) includes routers, firewall, switches, Wi-Fi access points, etc.

High Speed Connectivity

Our high speed connectivity enables a network connection devoted to your premise. We provide a managed, high performance and consistent speed connectivity with 24x7 monitoring & support.

The connectivity can be via Metro Ethernet, Broadband or Wireless Connectivity.

DIA & DDOS

Our Dedicated Internet Access (DIA) provides a dedicated connectivity to your premise. With DIA, The connection is symmetrical where you will enjoy upload speed same as download speed.

Our DDOS protection solution secures network while ensuring legitimate traffic is not compromised.

IPVPN

Our IPVPN service enables a private network (point to point or point to multipoint) between an HQ with it branches. The network can carry voice, data and video on a single network.

The connectivity can be via Ethernet, ADSL Broadband or wireless Connectivity (3G/4G). Staff will be able to access resources as if they are in the same office.

Cloud Services

Our cloud services provides the options to business owners to mi and match services over the cloud, independent of operating system.

Our services include Infrastructure As A Service (IAAS), Platform As A Service (PAAS) and Software As A Service (SAAS). The services are scalable, pay as you use basis with rapid implementation.

Security Services

Our security services are designed to protect company infrastructures and devices from unwanted attacks and unknown access.

We will assess client infrastructures as well as connections, evaluate its security level and propose firewall as well as other protective tools and measures. With the implementation, we can provide overall security health and visibility.

Data Center Colocation

Our data center colocation service allows business owners to rent space for servers and other hardware. We will provide, cooling, power, bandwidth, security and owners will provide servers and storage.

Besides colocation, other flavors of services include business continuity, hosting, backup, etc.

SDWAN

Our SDWAN service enables telecom services over the internet with the aid of software.

In includes connectivity and device delivery and installation. We also include 24x7 monitoring and maintenance services. Our service allows load sharing of traffic across multiple wan connections making data transmission more efficient.
